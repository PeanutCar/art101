Making a Youtube Video

  1. Create a Google account and Youtube account
  2. Set up your environment
  3. Set up the camera
  4. Collect the footage you need
  5. Upload the footage to a computer
  6. Import the footage into your video editing software
  7. Edit the video by cutting footage up and adding sound
  8. Export the finished product
  9. Go to YouTube and upload the video
  10. Give a description and other fields of info
  11. Publish the video
